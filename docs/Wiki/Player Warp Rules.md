## Current Rules

- **Claimed Locations**  
  PWarps must be claimed before anyone can use them.

- **Safety Requirements**  
  - No hostile mob spawns in the warp area.  
  - Grinder PWarps must have mobs safely contained.

- **Allowed PWarp Types**  
  - Shop  
  - Grinder  
  - Town

- **Visitor Safety**  
  All PWarp builds **MUST** be safe and easily navigable for guests.
